Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 31, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes our policies and procedures regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of your information when using the Charity A Day app and explains your privacy rights.

Data Collection

The Charity A Day app does not collect, store, or share any personal information from users. All content within the app, such as trivia questions, charity information, or sponsor details, is provided directly and is not linked to individual users.

Children's Privacy

The app does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from anyone under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with the updated "Last updated" date.
